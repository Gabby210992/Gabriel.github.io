Heart Disease Prediction using Machine Learning

In this project, I aimed to investigate the complex conditions surrounding heart disease, a leading cause of morbidity and mortality worldwide. With the increasing availability of electronic health records and advancements in machine learning algorithms, I developed a predictive model that can accurately forecast the likelihood of heart disease based on a set of attributes. By leveraging a comprehensive dataset, my goal is to identify the most relevant factors contributing to heart disease and create a reliable prediction tool that can assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions. This report outlines our methodology, results, and insights gained from this project.
